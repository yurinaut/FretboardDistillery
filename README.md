# Fretboard Distillery

**Transpose any song · Keep the shapes you love · Let the capo do the math**

Fretboard Distillery is a free, browser-based guitar transposition tool built for how guitarists actually work. No install, no account, no subscription — just open it and play.

🎸 **[Use it now → fretboarddistillery.github.io](https://fretboarddistillery.github.io)**

---

## Features

### Transpose with Shapes + Capo
Pick the **Sounding Key** the audience hears, then pick the **Shape Key** your fingers play (A, B, C, D, E, F, or G families). The capo position calculates automatically. Or nudge the capo manually and the sounding key adjusts to match.

### Nashville Number System
Toggle chord names to scale-degree numbers (1, 4, 5⁻, etc.) with a single click. Numbers stay the same in every key — great for bands that communicate in numbers. Includes a full NNS reference table for all 12 keys.

### Chord Diagrams & Simplified Voicings
Every chord in your song displays a mini fretboard diagram. Common chords include a simpler alternative (like Cadd9 for C, Em7 for Em, Fmaj7 for F) with an explanation of why it's easier.

### Chord Position Editor
Click any chord and use arrow keys, Space, or Backspace to slide it left or right — lining it up precisely over the syllable where the change happens.

### Performance Mode
Full-screen chord sheet with adjustable auto-scroll (0.1–1.5 speed), a 15-second countdown to get your hands on the guitar, and an optional metronome with adjustable BPM (40–200). The metronome starts immediately when you hit Play so you can count yourself in.

### Smart Print Preview
Auto-fits your song to the fewest pages possible. Choose between 1 or 2 column layouts, adjust font size (9–12pt), line spacing, and optionally include chord diagrams on the printout. Chord+lyric pairs never split across pages.

### Save & Reopen
Save your work as a `.fba` file that stores the song, transpose settings, Nashville mode, and any position edits. Reopen it anytime to pick up where you left off.

### File Import
Open files from your computer — supports `.txt`, `.cho` / `.chordpro`, `.docx` (Microsoft Word), and `.fba` (Fretboard Distillery native format).

### How To Use & Feature Guide
Built-in step-by-step instructions and feature reference accessible from the ☰ menu. No manual needed.

## Getting Started

1. Paste your chord sheet (chords on the line above lyrics) or open a file
2. Click **Parse & Load Song**
3. Pick your favorite shapes under **Play Shapes In**
4. Adjust the **Sounding Key** or **Capo** to your liking
5. Fine-tune chord positions with **✎ Edit Positions** if needed
6. Hit **🖨 Print** for a clean chord sheet or **▶** for Performance Mode

## Technical Details

- Single self-contained HTML file — no server, no dependencies, no tracking
- Works in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Fully responsive for desktop and tablet (iPad)
- All processing happens locally in your browser — your songs never leave your device
